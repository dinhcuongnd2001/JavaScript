Array method:
    1. foreach(): dung duyet qua tung phan tu cua mang
    2. every(): kiem tra tat ca cac phan tu cua mang co thoa man dk nao 
       day ko (return true or false)
    3. some(): kiem tra xem trong mang co 1 pt nao do thoa man dieu kien 
       hay ko
    4.find(): Lap mang de kiem tra xem co phan tu nao thoa man dieu kien 
      khong, neu co tra ve phan tu dau tien thoa man, neu khong tra ve 
      undefined
    5. filter(): giong find nhung tra ve danh sach cac phan tu thoa man
